International Quant Championship 2024 - Worldquant

June 2024

Overview

Local competition product portfolio higher than the market with less volatility. Launched and crystallized Alpha Function by using Python to filter out IR less than 1 for single-factor cumulative return less than 0.25 for any rolling 5-day period.

Results

Portfolio: XY-169452, IR 2.686, Sharpe 3.022 | National championship product portfolio higher than the market with less volatility. As a local champion, we were one of the selected top performers presenting to the regional judges.

Technical Approach

  • Developed custom Python algorithms to analyze performance metrics
  • Implemented filtering based on Information Ratio and cumulative return
  • Created a systematic approach to strategy evaluation and selection
  • Applied statistical methods to optimize portfolio construction

Key Technologies

Python Alpha Signals Quantitative Finance Statistical Analysis Portfolio Optimization

Learning Outcomes

Through this competition, I gained hands-on experience in quantitative finance methodologies, advanced my Python programming skills for financial applications, and developed a deeper understanding of alpha generation and portfolio construction techniques.

The opportunity to present our strategies to industry professionals provided valuable insights into real-world applications of quantitative investing approaches and reinforced the importance of robust strategy validation.
