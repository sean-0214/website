Open Data Hackathon 2024-25 - Hong Kong Techathon 2025

March 2025

Overview

Participated in the Hong Kong Techathon 2025, a data-driven hackathon focused on creating innovative solutions for urban challenges using open data sets provided by the Hong Kong government and private sector partners.

Project Description

Our team developed "UrbanInsight," a data visualization and analytics platform that provides actionable insights into city infrastructure, resource allocation, and public service optimization.

The platform integrated multiple data sources including transportation metrics, energy consumption patterns, and public service utilization to create a comprehensive urban dashboard for policy makers and city planners.

My Contributions

  • Led the financial data integration component of the project
  • Developed statistical models to predict resource allocation efficiency
  • Created interactive data visualizations using Python and D3.js
  • Presented the financial impact analysis to the judging panel

Technologies Used

Python Data Analysis Data Visualization D3.js Statistical Modeling Open Data APIs

Outcomes

Our project was recognized for its innovative approach to integrating financial and operational data for urban planning. Key achievements included:

  • Finalist position in the Urban Innovation track
  • Recognition for "Best Financial Impact Analysis"
  • Invitation to present our solution to the Hong Kong Smart City Office
  • Development of a proof-of-concept that continued beyond the hackathon

Lessons Learned

This hackathon reinforced the importance of cross-disciplinary collaboration, rapid prototyping, and the value of financial analysis in technology projects. It also provided hands-on experience with large-scale data integration challenges and real-time visualization techniques.
