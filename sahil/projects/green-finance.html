Green Finance - Bank of America Business Administration Paper 2022

October 2022

Overview

Conducted independent research on China-related Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) strategies for Bank of America Merrill Lynch. Analyzed key sustainable finance methodologies and their applications in the evolving investment landscape.

Research Focus

The project analyzed 10+ issuers, comparing policies under the Carbon Neutrality Initiative and examining the impact on funding premiums via a comparative study with conventional corporate bonds. Findings provided actionable insights for financial institutions adapting to sustainable finance trends.

Key Areas of Investigation

  • ESG integration in Chinese financial markets
  • Comparative analysis of green bond versus conventional bond premiums
  • Policy impacts of the Carbon Neutrality Initiative on corporate financing
  • Sustainable finance methodologies across different sectors
  • ESG reporting standards and their effectiveness

Methodologies

ESG Analysis Sustainable Finance Bond Market Research Policy Impact Assessment Comparative Analysis

Research Outcomes

The research identified several key trends in China's evolving ESG landscape, including:

  • Growing premium for green bonds compared to conventional bonds of similar ratings
  • Increasing standardization of ESG reporting among large Chinese corporations
  • Sector-specific variations in ESG implementation and outcomes
  • Emerging opportunities for financial institutions in sustainable finance advisory

The findings contributed to Bank of America Merrill Lynch's strategic approach to sustainable finance in Asian markets and provided actionable insights for institutional investors considering ESG integration in their portfolios.
