Point 72 x Fidelity x AIBC Asset Management Competition 2023

December 2023

Overview

Conducted FX analysis and presented on Natural Gas over Brent Oil as a more cost efficient source of power generation by spotting regime changes and analyzing against 5 macroeconomic factors on Bloomberg Terminal risk factors.

Key Contributions

Contributed to cryptocurrency sections within the portfolio, reaching semi-finalist status. Highlighted the need for digital asset allocation as a hedge against inflation and for portfolio diversification benefits.

Analysis Methodology

  • Utilized Bloomberg Terminal to research macroeconomic factors affecting energy markets
  • Performed regime change analysis to identify market shifts and opportunities
  • Conducted comparative studies between traditional energy sources and alternatives
  • Researched cryptocurrency allocation strategies for institutional portfolios

Key Technologies

Bloomberg Terminal FX Analysis Energy Markets Cryptocurrency Portfolio Construction

Outcomes

The competition provided valuable hands-on experience with professional financial analysis tools and methodologies. Our team reached semi-finalist status, demonstrating the strength of our analytical approach and portfolio construction strategy.

The research on cryptocurrency allocation as a portfolio diversification tool was particularly well-received and highlighted emerging trends in institutional asset management practices.
